You can request your full device allocation if you have 15 or more children who are self-isolating having been exposed to a confirmed coronavirus case outside the school community.

What to put in your request

Tell us:

  • how many children are self-isolating at the same time, having been exposed to a confirmed coronavirus case outside the school community
  • the year groups of the children who are self-isolating
  • if you’ve spoken to your health protection team about rates of transmission in your area

Where to send your request

Send your request in an email to:
COVID.TECHNOLOGY@education.gov.uk

What happens next

We’ll email you back with instructions about what to do next.
