Help children get online by giving them 4G wireless routers or requesting extra data for their mobile devices. You can request these for disadvantaged children:

  • in years 3 to 11 who do not have access to the internet and whose face-to-face education is disrupted. (In this event, you will also have been invited to order laptops and tablets).
  • who are extremely clinically vulnerable and need to shield on current official advice (this could be from a doctor or hospital consultant)
  • who live in a household that’s been advised to shield because a family member is clinically extremely vulnerable
  • who cannot attend school – even though theirs is open – because they live in a different area with local coronavirus restrictions

Request extra data for mobile devices

Request data for children who:

  • have access to a mobile device on one of the participating networks
  • can use that device to connect to the internet during school hours

Request 4G wireless routers

Request 4G wireless routers for:

  • children without access to a mobile device
  • families with multiple children needing internet access
