Broadband for families who recieve Universal Credit

Virgin media (opens in a new tab) is providing broadband for £15 per month for families who recieve Universal Credit.

Request internet access now

Make a request for internet access now for each pupil who does not have:

  • fixed broadband at home
  • a mobile phone or cannot afford data

For each pupil we can increase their or their family’s mobile phone data allowance if they are on a participating network.

If they do not have access to a phone to tether to or its not on a participating network we can provide the pupil with a wireless 4G router.

As soon as your school experiences a disruption to face-to-face education we’ll provide the connectivity you’ve requested.
