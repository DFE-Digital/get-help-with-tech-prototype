Your allocation

You’ve been allocated 300 log-ins to pass on to schools and families. A log-in is a username and password. BT calls these ‘vouchers’.

Your allocation was calculated based on school census data and free school meal (FSM) data. The Department for Education has a total of 10,000 BT log-ins to distribute across England.

Distributing log-ins

Not everyone in need will be able to access a BT hotspot, so schools should find out who the vouchers will work for before giving them out.
