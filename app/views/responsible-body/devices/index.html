Decide if schools can order their own devices

You can choose who orders devices if there's disruption to face to face education. You can:

  • let schools place their own orders

or

  • place some, or all, orders centrally

We recommend letting schools place orders because it’s the quickest way to get help to those who need it.

You’ll need to nominate someone in each school for us to contact.

Each contact will be able to:

  • manage who can order devices
  • order devices themselves

Help schools prepare for disruption

Give us contact details as soon as you can so we can help schools prepare to deliver remote education if they need to.

Orders can only be placed when local coronavirus restrictions are confirmed

We’ll contact people as soon as they’re able to place orders.

Orders placed before 4pm will normally be delivered to schools the next working day.

Each school has their own allocation

Every school has its own provisional allocation of laptops and tablets. Allocations are based on free school meal data and estimates of how many devices a school has already received.
