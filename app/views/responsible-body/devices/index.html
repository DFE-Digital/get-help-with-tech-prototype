Schools can now order their own devices

Schools will be able to order their own devices in the event of a local coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

We recommend you let schools order their own devices because this is the quickest way to get help to those who need it.

Prepare before a lockdown

Give us school details as soon as you can so we can contact them, and they can prepare before a local lockdown.

Orders can only be placed during a lockdown

Schools that are closed or partially closed because of a local lockdown will be notified that they can now place orders for devices.

Orders placed before 4pm will be delivered to schools the next working day.

Each school has their own allocation

Every school has its own allocation of devices. Allocations are based on free school meals and estimates of the number of devices a school already has.

Numbers will be re-assessed at the time of ordering based on availability and the extent of coronavirus outbreaks.
