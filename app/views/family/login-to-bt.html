To log in they should:

  • open their internet browser (for example Internet Explorer, Google Chrome or Firefox)
  • type www.btwifi.com:8443 in the address bar and press Enter
  • accept cookies by clicking Ok
  • they’ll see a page with prices on, they can ignore these as their internet access is free
  • when asked ‘Are you a BT or Fon customer?’ answer BT Wi-fi
  • enter the provided username and password

They can text SCHOOL to 12345 to receive a connection guide.
