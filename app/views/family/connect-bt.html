To connect to a BT Wifi network they should:

  • click on the wi-fi icon on their device
  • look for a network named BTWi-fi or _BTWi-fi from the list of available networks
  • click Connect

They can tick ‘Connect automatically’ to avoid repeating this step next time.

They can text SCHOOL to 12345 to receive a connection guide.
