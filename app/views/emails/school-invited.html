Ordering laptops and tablets

You and all other nominated contacts within your school will receive:

  • sign in details to the TechSource platform for placing orders
  • sign in details for the Computacenter Support Portal for technical assistance
  • an email when the ordering process devices become available as a result of local coronavirus (COVID-19) restrictions

Email COVID.TECHNOLOGY@education.gov.uk if you need to place an order because your school:

  • supports a clinically extremely vulnerable child who is shielding or self-isolating following official advice
  • is supporting children who live in an area subject to local restrictions

Devices will be delivered directly to your school within 2 working days of an order being confirmed.
