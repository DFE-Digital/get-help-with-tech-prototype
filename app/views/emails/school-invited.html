The Department for Education (DfE) is providing laptops and tablets to help children and families access remote education during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.

Get help with technology

Your local authority ({{ data.rb }}) has given you access to the Get help with technology service. You can use this to:

  • tell us who will order devices for your school
  • access TechSource to order devices during a lockdown

You will receive a separate email from Computacentre with a username and password for TechSource.

Sign in

Sign in using your email address ({{ to }}) to get started:

https://get-help-with-tech.education.gov.uk/sign-in

Bookmark this page, you will need it if there is a local lockdown.

Tell us who will order your devices

Use this service to give us details of people in your school who will be responsible for placing orders.

Everyone will receive:

  • access to this service to add and edit who can make orders in their school
  • access to the TechSource system for placing orders
  • detailed instructions for how to place an order in the event of a local coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak that affects their school(s) or pupils
  • notifications in the event of a local lockdown
