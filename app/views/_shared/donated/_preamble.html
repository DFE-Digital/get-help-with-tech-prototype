Mail Force are collecting:

  • donations to buy new laptops
  • used laptops donated by companies

Donated laptops are being checked, refurbished if used, and distributed to schools and colleges.

About the laptops

Every laptop will meet our minimum requirements, but they are not all the same.

All laptops will be Microsoft Windows devices. They are provided with factory settings and you should configure them at your own cost before lending them to children and young people.
