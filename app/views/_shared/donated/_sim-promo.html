The charity campaign Mail Force Computers for Kids and Vodafone are offering 150,000 SIM cards to schools and colleges.

Each SIM card comes with 30GB of data that must be used within 90 days.

You can request up to 50 SIM cards. They are available on a first come first served basis.

If you want to make a request, you will need to:

  • visit the Vodafone website
  • find your school or college using its postcode
  • give headteacher details
  • choose how many SIM cards you want
