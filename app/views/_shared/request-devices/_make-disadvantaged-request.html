  • why they need the devices

If shielding is not recommended in your area (see the guidance on local restrictions), you’ll need to show evidence that children have received official advice to continue shielding outside of national guidelines.

If you are requesting routers

Try requesting extra mobile data first. Compare routers and mobile data requests to check which is best for a household.

We have a limited number of routers available and may not be able to give you the number you request.

To help us, please give evidence of:

  • how you’ve identified disadvantage, such as the number of children receiving free school meals or pupil premium
  • your understanding of children’s access to the internet
  • disadvantaged pupils joining the school since the Schools Census

Evidence can be surveys, calls home, awareness of families, demographic data, or questions asked to parents. Do not send personal information, like names of children or dates of birth.
