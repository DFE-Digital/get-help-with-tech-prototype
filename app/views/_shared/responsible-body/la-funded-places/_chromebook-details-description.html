• the domain the educational settings has registered for Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals (formerly G Suite for Education), for example ‘school.co.uk’
  • a recovery email address. This should be on a separate domain to the one registered for Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals. For example, if the registered domain is ‘school.com’, the recovery email cannot be ‘recovery@school.com’
