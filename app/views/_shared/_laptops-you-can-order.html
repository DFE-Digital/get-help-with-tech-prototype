Depending on availability, you can choose from:

Standard Windows laptops

You’ll be responsible for arranging and maintaining safeguarding measures, like content filtering.

DfE Restricted Windows laptops

These have temporary safeguarding settings installed - content filtering and mobile device management software - which you’ll need to replace before they expire on 30 September 2021. You’ll need to reset these devices before you can apply new settings, or download some types of software.

Google Chromebooks

You’ll need to provide us with the relevant school’s email domain for Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals (formerly G Suite for Education), and a recovery email address.

{% elif data.features.socialCare %}

Ordering for care leavers in education

After you order, we'll ask you for each educational setting's email domain for Google Workspace for Education Fundamentals (formerly G Suite for Education), and a recovery email address. Each setting's IT team should be able to give you this information.

Ordering for care leavers who are not in education

If we cannot link a Chromebook to a Google Workspace for Education domain, it will not be secure. Your LA can choose to:

  • order a Windows laptop instead
  • set up and manage a Google domain
  • distribute the device unsecured - read our guidance on safeguarding before making this choice
